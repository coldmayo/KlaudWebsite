About KlaudOS

KlaudOS is a meme operating system made from scratch (not a Linux distro or anything of that nature) that was completely made in C and x86 Assembly. It has a 2 stage boot loader and a CLI.

Who's Klaud and why does he have an operating system?

Klaud is a character in the sequel Star Wars films who stole the hearts of millions. He fought for the Resistance and was a talented mechanic. He has an operating system because Klaud deserves one as the greatest Star Wars character of all time.
