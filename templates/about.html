About KlaudOS

KlaudOS is a meme operating system made from scratch (not a Linux distro or anything like that). It is completely made in C and x86 Assembly and it has a double stage bootloader with an interactive kernel.

Who's Klaud and why does he have an OS?

Klaud was a character in Star Wars Episode 9. I remember the day I saw him in theaters and his character really stuck with me. Years later I was introduced to Operating System development and TempleOS which inspired me further. So, I decided I would give back to Klaud by creating this hobby operating system years later.

Features

If you want an updated features list visit docs/commands.md or run klaud --help in the KlaudOS kernel.
